# 🏦 Bangladesh Banking Landscapes Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the banking landscape of Bangladesh using **Power BI**. It covers branch distribution, market share, geographic coverage, and regional concentration across all divisions, districts, and thanas of the country.

---

## 📈 Key Features of Dashboard
- 🏦 Total Banks, Branches, Divisions, Districts & Thanas — KPI Summary Cards
- 📊 Branch distribution across all **8 divisions** (bar chart)
- 🌳 Top 10 banks by branch count (treemap)
- 📍 Top 10 thanas with highest branch concentration (line chart)
- 🥧 Bank market share by branch distribution (pie chart)
- 🗺️ Geographic bank branch map across Bangladesh
- 🔍 Interactive division-level slicer and cross-filtering

---

## 🧠 Insights Derived
- **Dhaka** leads all divisions with ~4,000+ branches, followed by Chattogram and Rajshahi
- **Sonali Bank PLC** holds the largest market share at **10.8%** (1.21K branches)
- **Motijheel** thana has the densest branch concentration with **209 branches**
- **Bangladesh Krishi Bank** ranks 2nd with **9.08%** share, reflecting strong rural outreach
- All **8 divisions** are covered: Dhaka, Chattogram, Rajshahi, Khulna, Sylhet, Rangpur, Barishal & Mymensingh
- Total banking network spans **64 districts** and **595 thanas**
